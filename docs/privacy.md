RapidMaternal is a coordination and notification system, not a clinical record system.

## Data Minimization
The system collects only the minimum data required to support real-time maternal emergency coordination.

## Excluded Data
RapidMaternal does not store patient identifiers, clinical diagnoses, treatment details, or personal medical information.

## Purpose Limitation
All data collected is used solely for emergency response coordination, accountability, and system improvement.

## Access Control
Access is restricted to authorized health workers and district officials based on role.

## Phase 1 Scope
These boundaries apply strictly to Phase 1 and will not be expanded without formal review and approval.
